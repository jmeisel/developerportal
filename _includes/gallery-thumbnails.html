Applications Currently Using ITA's Data

BusinessUSA helps small businesses find the government resources they need. Their Exporting section automatically populates its pages using ITA's Trade Events and Offices & Centers APIs. Just click Change Location and enter a new zip code to see the results.
ThinkGlobal, a B2B trade leads platform, helps U.S. exporters find overseas buyers and distributors. ThinkGlobal has developed an *Export Resources* app for both Android and Apple iOS devices. The mobile apps and the Export USA magazine home page provide Trade Resources for U.S. companies by displaying the most current Trade Leads, Market Research Reports, Trade News, and Trade Events from ITA.
FedEx has just updated its Small Business Center and includes ITA's Trade Events in its Export Education Events pages. Now it’s easier than ever to find the specific knowledge a business needs to go global more strategically and successfully. Through FedEx's export education program, get a direct connection to many practical and insightful export-specific educational programs throughout the year, offered across the U.S.
